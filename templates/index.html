F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Some of the common doubts

  • What cleaning products do Quick Cleaning use?

    We use natural and chemical-free cleaning products for the cleaning process. We also don't repeat the used cleaning tools for another booking.

  • Is Frontline Cleanrrs available on weekends?

    Yes,Frontline Cleaning Services are available in Weekends. If you need the cleaning service at the weekends may be after a party, don’t hesitate to contact us. Our professional cleaners will reach your doorstep and will do the job for you.

  • How often should you hire a cleaning company?

    You can hire a cleaning company when you need. If you need a regular cleaning services, then you can hire the professional cleaners from Frontline Cleaners, So our professional cleaners will come to your place regularly for cleaning.

  • How many hours do I need?

    It depends on the size of building, how many rooms and how much is in them, plus the quality and depth of cleaning that you require. The choice is yours, and you may want fewer or more hours and then prioritise the rooms and jobs to be completed.

  • How do you vet the cleaners?

    We first carry out a telephone interview. If the applicant seems suitable, we then arrange for a more thorough interview to take place in their own home. Only after taking proof of ID and address along with references do we accept them onto our database. We then monitor every cleaner thoroughly for the whole time they are with us.

  • Can I change my cleaner if I am not happy?

    Yes, of course, we have a large and varied cleaner base, and we'd be happy to assign you someone different if you are unhappy for any reason. Just give us a call.
